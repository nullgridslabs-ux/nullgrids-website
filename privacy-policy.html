PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: V1.0 // 2026

1. Information Collection

When you register as a contributor, partner, or participant in NULLGRIDS operations (including NCWC), we collect necessary identification and contact information. This may include your name, educational institution, email address, and technical skill set.

2. Data Usage & Telemetry

Your data is used strictly for operational coordination, event registration, and communication regarding NULLGRIDS initiatives. We do not sell your personal data to third-party brokers. Participant performance metrics during Capture The Flag (CTF) events may be logged for scoring and leaderboards.

3. Communication Protocols

By providing your email address, you consent to receive operational briefings, event updates, and partnership communications. You may opt out of non-critical communications at any time by contacting our command center.

4. Security Posture

We implement standard security measures to protect the information you submit. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure. Participants engaging in our CTF platforms are reminded to use dedicated credentials and not reuse passwords from critical personal accounts.

5. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding this privacy policy or to request data removal, contact us at: nullgridslabs@gmail.com
