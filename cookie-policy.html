COOKIE POLICY

LAST UPDATED: V1.0 // 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us deliver a functional and consistent experience. This policy explains how NULLGRIDS Labs uses cookies and similar technologies across our web properties.

2. Cookies We Use

We use a minimal set of cookies strictly necessary for platform operation:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
session_id Strictly Necessary Maintains your session on the CTF platform Session
csrf_token Strictly Necessary Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks Session
theme_pref Functional Remembers your display preferences 30 days

3. What We Do NOT Use

NULLGRIDS Labs does not use:

  • Third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.
  • Cross-site behavioral profiling.
  • Social media tracking cookies (e.g., Facebook Pixel).
  • Analytics tools that collect personally identifiable information without consent.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling strictly necessary cookies may impact platform functionality such as login sessions on the CTF platform. Refer to your browser's documentation for instructions on managing cookies.

For questions about our cookie practices, contact: nullgridslabs@gmail.com
