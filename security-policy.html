SECURITY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: V1.0 // 2026

1. Our Security Commitment

NULLGRIDS Labs is a cybersecurity organization and takes the security of our platforms, data, and participants with the utmost seriousness. We implement industry-standard controls across all operational infrastructure including our CTF platforms, cyber ranges, and web properties.

2. Infrastructure Security

Our platforms operate with layered security controls including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption on all web properties and API endpoints.
  • Network segmentation between competition infrastructure and administrative systems.
  • Access controls with least-privilege principles for all team members.
  • Regular security reviews of all externally facing services.
  • Isolated environments for CTF challenge infrastructure to prevent lateral movement.

3. Data Protection

Participant and partner data is stored with appropriate encryption at rest. We do not store payment card information. Credentials and sensitive data are never logged in plaintext. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Incident Response

In the event of a security incident affecting our platforms or participant data, NULLGRIDS Labs will:

  • Contain and assess the incident within 24 hours of detection.
  • Notify affected participants within 72 hours if personal data is involved.
  • Document the incident, root cause, and remediation steps.
  • Publish a post-incident report for transparency where appropriate.

5. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to all NULLGRIDS Labs owned and operated services including nullgridslabs.dpdns.org and blackcipher.nullgridslabs.dpdns.org. For reporting vulnerabilities, see our Responsible Disclosure Policy.

6. Contact

For security-related concerns, contact us at: nullgridslabs@gmail.com with the subject line [SECURITY].
